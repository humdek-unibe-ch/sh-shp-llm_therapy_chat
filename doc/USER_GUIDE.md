## 1. What This System Is

This system combines **AI-powered supportive conversations** with **therapist monitoring and intervention capabilities**.

### Purpose

- **Extend therapeutic support between sessions** - not replace professional care
- Provide patients with 24/7 access to empathetic, evidence-based support
- Give therapists full oversight, control, and authority over patient care
- Enable timely intervention when human support is needed

## Clinical Boundaries

### Clear & Explicit Safeguards

| Statement | Explanation |
|-----------|-------------|
| **The AI is NOT a therapist** | It provides supportive responses, not clinical treatment |
| **The AI does NOT diagnose** | No medical or psychological diagnoses are made |
| **The AI does NOT replace therapy** | It supplements, never substitutes professional care |
| **The AI does NOT act as sole crisis care** | Human escalation is built-in for emergencies |
| **The therapist remains the primary clinical decision-maker** | Full oversight and control |

### Patient Disclaimers

Patients see clear disclaimers:
- On first use
- At regular intervals during conversations
- When the AI detects concerning content

## 2. How It Works for Patients

### AI Chat Buddy (24/7)

Patients can chat anytime with a supportive AI trained in evidence-based mental health best practices:

- **Empathy** - Understanding and validating feelings
- **Validation** - Acknowledging emotions without judgment
- **Grounding** - Techniques for managing distress
- **Reflection** - Helping patients process thoughts

### Tagging Their Therapist

When patients feel human support is needed, they can tag their therapist directly:

**How to tag:**
1. Click the "Tag Therapist" button
2. Select a reason (optional):
   - "I'm feeling overwhelmed"
   - "I need to talk soon"
   - "This feels urgent"
   - "Emergency - please respond immediately"
3. The therapist receives an immediate notification

## 3. Therapist Features Overview

### Core Capabilities

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Real-time Dashboard** | Monitor all patient conversations in one place |
| **Risk Level Indicators** | Visual alerts for concerning conversations |
| **AI Control** | Enable/disable AI responses per conversation |
| **Smart Alerts** | Notifications for tags, danger, high activity |
| **Clinical Notes** | Add private notes to conversations |
| **Full History** | Access complete conversation history |
| **Invisible Monitoring** | Observe without patient knowing |
| **Easy Intervention** | One-click to join any conversation |

### Conversation Control

You can:
- **Pause AI responses** in a specific conversation
- **Switch to therapist-only mode** temporarily
- **Resume AI support** at any time
- **Set risk levels** (Low/Medium/High/Critical)
- **Add private notes** not visible to patients

## 5. AI Control & Customization

### Per-Conversation Settings

For each conversation, you can control:

| Setting | Options | Purpose |
|---------|---------|---------|
| **AI Enabled** | On / Off | Whether AI responds automatically |
| **Mode** | AI Hybrid / Human Only | Default conversation mode |
| **Risk Level** | Low / Medium / High / Critical | Manual risk assessment |
| **Status** | Active / Paused / Closed | Conversation status |

### When to Pause AI

Consider pausing AI when:
- Patient is discussing trauma in detail
- Sensitive topics requiring clinical judgment
- Patient explicitly requests human-only support
- Danger keywords detected
- Complex emotional situations

### When to Keep AI Active

AI works well for:
- Routine check-ins
- Grounding exercises
- General supportive conversation
- After-hours support between sessions
- Initial engagement before therapist responds

## 8. Intervening in Conversations

### Easy Intervention

**One-click to join any conversation:**

1. Select conversation from list
2. Type your response
3. Click "Send Response"
4. Your message appears under your name (never as AI)

### Seamless Handoff

When you join a conversation:

1. AI performs **seamless handoff** and steps back
2. Patient sees your message clearly labeled with your name
3. AI can remain active (hybrid mode) or pause automatically

### Group Communication

You can send messages to:
- **Individual patients** - Direct conversation
- **Entire therapy groups** - Group announcements

Group messages clearly identify therapist outreach vs. individual responses.

## 10. Therapist Daily Workflow

### Morning Check-In

1. **Log into the dashboard**
2. **Review overnight activity** - Check for new messages, tags
3. **Address urgent alerts** - Critical/Emergency first
4. **Acknowledge tags** - Let patients know you've seen their request

### During the Day

1. **Monitor conversations** - Live or periodic check
2. **Respond within normal clinical response time**
3. **Intervene when needed:**
   - Crisis situations
   - Complex emotional needs
   - Boundary-sensitive topics
4. **Add clinical notes** as needed

### Evening Review

1. **Review daily summaries** - Message activity
2. **Identify follow-ups** - Patients needing next-day attention
3. **Check safety reports** - Any danger detections
4. **Review trend indicators** - Mood patterns, escalation frequency

### Recommended Schedule

| Time | Activity |
|------|----------|
| Start of day | Review alerts, acknowledge tags |
| Throughout | Periodic monitoring, interventions |
| Lunch | Quick check for urgent items |
| End of day | Review summaries, plan follow-ups |

## 11. Workload & Availability Protection

### Therapist Controls

You define:
- **Office hours** - When you're actively monitoring
- **After-hours behavior** - What triggers alerts
- **Immediate vs. next-day alerts** - Alert routing

### How AI Helps Reduce Workload

The AI:
- **Handles routine emotional support** - Validation, reflection
- **Encourages self-regulation skills** - Grounding, breathing
- **Escalates only when clinically appropriate** - Smart thresholds
- **Provides 24/7 coverage** - No therapist burnout from constant availability

### Preventing Burnout

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| AI handles routine support | Reduces message volume requiring response |
| Smart filtering | Only urgent items demand immediate attention |
| Configurable alerts | Control what triggers notifications |
| Invisible monitoring | Check on patients without engagement pressure |

### Data Protection

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Encryption** | All conversations encrypted in transit and at rest |
| **Access Control** | Role-based permissions (patient/therapist/admin) |
| **Audit Trails** | All actions logged with timestamps |
| **Data Retention** | Configurable retention policies |

### Compliance Considerations

The system supports:
- **Patient data privacy** - Strict access controls
- **Therapist-patient confidentiality** - Notes private to therapists
- **Audit requirements** - Full action logging
- **Emergency protocols** - Danger detection and escalation

### Security Features

- **Group-based access** - Therapists only see their patients
- **Conversation-level permissions** - Per-conversation access control
- **Invisible monitoring** - Review without patient awareness
- **Session management** - Automatic timeouts

### What's Logged

- All message sends (AI, therapist, patient)
- Risk level changes
- Status changes
- Tag acknowledgments
- Alert reads
- Note creation
